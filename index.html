who we are

Designed by Rtdd in Italy Assembled in China

Rtdd Machinery was founded in 2011 by several Italian engineers with years of experience in finishing machinery and

now we are a team of nearly 50 engineers and technicians who are living with passion and ideas. We spent the past

years for studying and developing better machines with advanced technology. We don’t make all machines, but we

make only better machines. All mechanical, software, and electronic elements are designed in Italy by Rtdd, and

tested in our Chinese Assembled Centre to ensure every product to be perfect when it reaches you.

Why

why rtdd

The strategy of sustainable development

We believe the very best finishing machines can produce not only great fabrics but as well as a better world. We are

determined to make the better machines and we always try our best to protect our environment. Reducing labor,

power, water, and other related energy are daily topics. However, aftersales service is one of our most important

jobs to face every day. We have built up a professional team to give our best service to you before, during, and after

purchasing our products. Come to us, we will lead you to a new way to business and a place with bluer sky.

Auto-Cooling

Maintain the brush at the room temperature is the key to the stable finishing quality.

Auto-Cleaning

18000m3/h air flow to remove your headache in cleaning.

Auto-Control

Fully automation is achieved at iSUEDE to reduce labor cost as much as possible.

No-man Entry

A patented system with automatically centering and opening function for both sides' curling for any kinds of fabrics.

Low tension exit

Machine Exit is designed for very low tension via conveyor belt.

Double Load Cell

iSUEDE's load cells are working as twins for each free roller in order to be very accurate and real, as well as to maximize the sensor life.

Fast-maintain

We experienced, thought, and tried our best to save your time and energy in maintenance in many aspects, for example, it takes only max. 30 mins to change the timing belts and you don't need to disassemble any brush in the drum to have the filament cut.

Drum in two

We separate the drum into two parts with individual tension control to avoid the tension difference between the entry and exit ofthe drum.

AMMONIA TREATMENT PLANT

BUILD YOUR OWN MULTIFUNCTIONAL AMMONIA TREATMENT PLANT

All fabrics with natural fiber will be suitable including shirting, denim, and knitting.

The only ammonia processing Centre with width and length control of the fabrics.

Smart drying system without any direct contact with fabric surface.

Low water consumption is specially designed for the RTDD ammonia.

99% of ammonia will be recovered and distillated.

The system will discharge only clean water and clean air both with 10 PPM of ammonia.

Distillation system with high performance.

Top safety system designed according to both the European & USA standard.

Max. Mechanical Speed will be 80m/min and Max. Operating Speed will be 60m/min according to the GSM of the fabrics.

No sulfuric acid is required at Rtdd’s recovery unit.

Much Lower power consumption is also what we worked hard to achieve.
